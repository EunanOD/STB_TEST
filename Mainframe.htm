Welcome to Derry Country Manor Hotel Derry

Perched on the banks of the River Foyle, within a five-minute walk from the historic Derry Walls, the 4 star Derry Country Manor Hotel Derry is ready to welcome you to one of Northern Ireland’s most idyllic locations.

Where the Wild Atlantic Way meets the Causeway Coastal Route, the Derry Country Manor Hotel is located with close proximity to some of the most stunning sightseeing destinations. Our 4* hotel location is the perfect base to explore the beauty of the north west, as well as some of the most remarkable golf courses in Ireland.

After you’ve finished exploring, you can relax in one of our 158 bedrooms, including "The Collection Penthouse Suites & Rooms", superior rooms, suites, family rooms and accessible rooms..
