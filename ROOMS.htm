Executive Junior Suites

Our opulent collection of exclusive rooms on the 7th floor, a mixture of luscious fabrics, indulgent textures and contemporary furnishing in each room, individually inspired by the City furnished to the highest levels of comfort and style with your enjoyment and relaxation as primary focus.
